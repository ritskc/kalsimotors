Insurance

At Peel Auto Collision, we will work directly with all insurance companies to help complete the repairs needed to meet manufacture specifications. It is your responsibility to choose a collision repair shop, not your insurance company’s.

Some insurance company’s may want you to visit their drive-in claims centre before having your car repaired. You can bring it to the auto body shop of your choice to have the car inspected by the insurance there instead of going to where they want you to go.

We will do our best to ensure all the details of the repair quotations and paperwork are handled accurately and we will work closely with your insurance broker & insurance company as well for all reporting and filing as necessary.

Our corporation employs and partners with only the best in the industry.
